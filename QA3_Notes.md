
# Fertilizer application

**Mumberes**:
Npk for maize 75kg, potatoes 100kg per acre respectively

*~90 t/ac NPK --> ~225 t/ha*

**Lanyuak**:
Depending on the crop. For cereals the farmers use 75 to 100kg per hectare and 250 to 500kg for horticultural crops particularly potatoes of either DAP, NPK and CAN/Urea.

*~200 t/ha of any fertilizer, on average, so splitting equally*

**Kabianga**:
NPK for maize/Potatoes-125kg per acre, CAN for tea- 100kg per acre

Assuming tea is 50% of crops, and maize/potatoes is other 50% (roughly):
*NPK: 125 kg/ac x 0.5 = 300 kg/ha x 0.5 = 150 kg/ha*
*CAN: 100 kg/ac x 0.5 = 250 kg/ha x 0.5 = 125 kg/ha*
